Munsyari? Which place is it?

I think you have heard about Munsyari from one of your friend or seen some amusable pics on insta or facebook.If you have never heard about this place then you should check an account on instagram named @voicesofmunsiari. It's in Uttarakhan's Pithoragarh district near china border. This is one of the most beautiful account on insta which is handled by the villagers themselves. There are 333 species of birds in Munsyari vally.


  Munsyari or Munsiari is situated on the 2500 meter elevation and also called the little Kashmir. one can see Panchachuli peaks from every corner of the munsyari. It looks like heaven when snow covers the whole area.


Places to visit in Munsyari

  • Mesar Kund


    Alka Rautela©


    This is Mesar Kund of Munsiari . It is very peaceful place the most important thing about this place is its beauty . I like to sit here and listen to the song of birds. Also it is good place for birdwatching . Many birds like barbets, drongos, eagles, woodpeckers, sapsuckers and many more other species of birds can be seen here easily. There are some tales about this pond. My Nani(granny) told that this pond was much larger than now it is, but year after year it started drying. There are two ponds. New one pond has been made by the villagers. On May every year we celebrate a festival called " Mesar wan koutik " . I like this fair too much . Please come and enjoy 'Mesar Wan Koutik ' . Also the beauty of Munsiari.

  • Nanda Devi Temple


    • Nanda devi temple is situated in Danadhar. It's a tourist place. From this place one can see panchchuli and Hansling easily, if weather isn't cloudy. We went for a trek to a hot water spring, the museum and Danadhar with all the guides. It was a nice experience.Here is a story about the formation of Nanda Devi Temple in this place. There was a merchant, who wanted to make a temple of Nanda Devi at his village. He was very ill. One day Nanda Devi came in his dream and said " place me at that place from where I can see everywhere." Then he told his dream to everyone and this temple was formed here.
